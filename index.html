Etch-A-Sketch

How To Sketch

  • Set your grid size from 1 - 100 and click the 'Create Grid' button
  • Click anywhere on the grid to start / stop sketching
  • Choose your pen color (default is white) or use 'Rainbow Pen'
  • Turn your 'Eraser' on / off and click anywhere on the grid to start / stop erasing
  • Change the color of your grid with the 'Grid Color' button
  • Change the color of your sketch to any color you want, even multi-color
  • Clear your grid with the 'Clear Grid' button
  • Try it out now!
